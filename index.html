A SAFER POINT OF VIEW

Who We Are

Kleen security is a cyber-security startup which is shortly called as KleenSec. It is a young startup in the field of cyber security which presently provides training in cyber-security. The clear vision of our company is to encourage and educate young Indian to secure their digital world.

What We Do

In this ever-expanding world of technology, where safety is now a myth. KleenSec provides you training on how you can protect yourself and keep yourself safe on the internet. A proper introduction to the cyber world is a basic need for everyone in this digital age so that people can learn how they can keep themselves safe and their data private. KleenSec helps to provide necessary introduction in this aspect of cyber security and internet.

Hackdot

Hackdot is an event or cyberfest conducted by Kleen security in and throughout different institutions and university. Hackdot is a multi-day event. It is a cyber-fest divided into 4 different modules that are focusing on information security and penetration testing. We aim that learning is done through both theoretical and practical practive.

Hackdot Objective

At the end of the fest,participants will be able to

  • Understand and perform information gathering
  • Understand Web Application Attacks and its Security
  • Understand and perform forensics
  • Understand and perform cryptography
  • Understand and perform steganography

Delivery Method

  • Hands on training

Fees Structure

  • Hackdot–complete 5 days fest–on premises training–800 INR
Module1-Seminar
  • We provide a head start with the basic information on how and where to start a persons’ cyber security goals with the involvement of industry experts and intelligence department
  • We have designed this module as an easygoing introduction of cyber security to a student and to provide a clear idea about the cyber world. The duration of this module is 3 hours
Module2-Workshop
  • This module deals with live ethical hacking session. We will cover basic to advance level of ethical hacking concepts and tools
  • Different types of cyber-attacks, tools and terminologies along with different hacking tools and system are discuss briefly in this workshop
  • It will be for a duration of 12 hours – 16 hour(8hours per day)
Module3-Training
  • How to use training and make an efficient team for CTF
  • We will provide free access to our CTF portal for solving challenges
Module4-CTF Challenge
  • The final stage of this cyber fest is a Capture the Flag challenge
  • This module will be after the training done in the third module
  • There will be several challenges on cyber security topics and different concepts
  • There will be score points give to each question submission
